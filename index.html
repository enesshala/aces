    Advanced Comfort Engineering Solution A C E S

    ACES is a limited liability company that is created by four shareholders who have a long experience in the area of engineering (resumes shown in the section of Staff). The company headquarters are located in Prishtina, where the company has the offices and the showroom of the products that it provides.

    ACES provides HVAC products and water supply products of international producers, mostly EU, in the area of Kosovo. As per HVAC products, the company is focused more in the renewable products, like Heat Pumps, Air Conditioning Inverters, biomass boilers and the likes. ACES is also providing other HVAC and Water supply products, like pipes, valves, manifolds, etc. from some European companies.

    Fresh Products Worth Ordering

    ACES is the official distributor of the Teknopoint for the area of Kosova and is also selling Tubiplast Products in Kosovo. The company is also having new cooperations every day, to sell and distribute many products of European companies in Kosovo. The long experience of the staff, in the area of management, installation and supervision of the HVAC and Plumbing, makes ACES a very serious and high potential company. ACES is very pleased to have in board Academic Staff, Architects, long experienced HVAC engineers, Masters of Water Supply and Plumbing area, designers and very qualified staff for installation of the products that it sells.
